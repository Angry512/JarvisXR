# Do Not Jailbreak XR 18.7.9

Do not attempt random jailbreak tools on this iPhone XR.

## Why

- iPhone XR is A12.
- iPhone XR is arm64e.
- checkm8 and palera1n-style routes do not apply to XR.
- iOS 18.7.9 has no verified XR jailbreak in the checked sources.

## Avoid

- YouTube jailbreaks.
- Paid unlockers.
- Survey jailbreaks.
- Browser jailbreaks.
- Fake Cydia installers.
- One click jailbreak claims.

## Trusted Sources Required

Before any future attempt, require:

- Official jailbreak project site.
- Respected jailbreak wiki.
- Reproducible GitHub release.
- Known developer announcement.
- Broad community validation.

If a jailbreak appears later, create a new feasibility pass before installing anything.
